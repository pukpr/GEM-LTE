Detailed source-reproduction calibration coefficients

    

      

      
The LTE pipeline here is used as a source-reproduction chain: (1) 31 tidal constituents summed as cos-phase waves with YEAR_LEN = 365.2472484 d; (2) the Ada-style drifting impulse comb from Amplify(...), using delB, bg, and year, modulates the tidal sum; (3) the bidirectional IIR integrator is seeded at IDATE = 1920.9; (4) an affine calibration aligns the reconstructed manifold to column 4; (5) MLR with regressors [1, m, sin/cos(2πk⋅m)] for k ∈ {23.24, 9.47, 75.73, 227.20} reproduces the source-model SLR output in column 2.

      

      

    

    

      Data notes
      
Warnemünde 2, Germany (54.17°N, 12.10°E), 1855.25–2024.17 monthly samples. Embedded series preserve the GEM-LTE column meanings from experiments/Feb2026/11/lte_results.csv: column 1 = date, column 2 = source-model SLR output, column 3 = observed 11.dat sea-level record, column 4 = internal forcing manifold level. Tidal constituents and calibration parameters come from experiments/Feb2026/11/lt.exe.p. The internal manifold reconstruction now follows the Ada forcing path more closely: delB sets the impulse month by truncation, while bg and year drift the impulse timing before the bidirectional IIR step. YEAR_LEN = 365.2472484 d = 365.2422484 + 0.005 (YEAR env from lt.exe.resp).
