MSL site: SEVASTOPOL, Ukraine. Coastal tide gauge at 44.62°N, 33.53°E on the Black Sea (Crimea).
Sevastopol is best known as a fiercely contested strategic naval base that has been at the center of major conflicts — including the Crimean War siege of 1854–1855, the WWII defense of 1941–1942, and the 2014 Russian annexation of Crimea.
