MSL site: WEST-TERSCHELLING, Netherlands. Coastal tide gauge at 53.36°N, 5.22°E on the Wadden Sea.
West-Terschelling is best known as the main village on Terschelling island, a popular Dutch holiday destination within the UNESCO World Heritage Wadden Sea, and as the host of the Oerol Festival — one of Europe's largest open-air theatre festivals.
